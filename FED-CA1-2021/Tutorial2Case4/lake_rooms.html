Accommodation

Our rooms and suites are the ideal choice for those who wish to be conveniently located the old centre of Athens, and also want to admire the historic side of the city at their leisure. Each and every one of the eighty-six guest-rooms and four mini suites of Lakescape Hotel are especially designed with great attention to detail, offer high aesthetics, functionality and comfort, and are suited to match your every need. Understated luxury and charming elegance define our quarters while our soothing colour palette and high grade materials are aimed at instilling the feeling of arriving at home, after a long day. Your temporary Athenian home, in the best neighbourhood in the entire city.
